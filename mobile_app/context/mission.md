# Mission

TaxiTrio is a smart tourist transportation and tour booking platform for international travelers in Cambodia. 

## Core Objectives
1. **Seamless Transportation:** Enable customers to browse verified taxis, intercity route packages, and tour packages.
2. **Transparent Booking:** Create and track bookings securely, choosing from multiple payment methods.
3. **Safety & Assistance:** Provide access to tourist assistance like emergency contacts, travel tips, and WhatsApp support.
4. **Driver Empowerment:** Allow drivers to manage their availability (online/offline), accept assigned bookings, update trip statuses, and track earnings transparently.

## Target Audience
- **International Travelers (Tourists):** People visiting Cambodia who need reliable, scam-free transportation and tours, often facing language barriers and currency issues.
- **Local Verified Drivers:** Professional drivers seeking a consistent stream of bookings and fair earnings.

## Value Propositions
- **Upfront Pricing:** No hidden fees or haggling.
- **Verified Drivers:** Vetted professionals to eliminate scam risks.
- **Clear Status Tracking:** Real-time updates on booking status.
- **Multilingual Support:** Breaking language barriers with multi-language app interfaces and WhatsApp support integration.
